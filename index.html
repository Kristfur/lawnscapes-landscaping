About Me

Hello, my name is Christopher, I am the founder of Lawnscapes Landscaping. From when I was a boy, I loved being outside in nature. I grew up camping in the wilderness and I fell in love with the beauty of nature. Since I landed my first landscaping job in 2019, I have been in love with transforming neglected, lawns into showcase gardens. I believe that plants deserve to look good, and with Lawnscapes Landscaping we can make that happen!

Why Choose Us?

We are a small, family run business who pride ourselves in working closely with our clients to provide them with a service that meets their needs.

Our staff are friendly, highly trained and professional, who work efficiently to make your garden perfect. Your friends will defiantly be talking about it.
